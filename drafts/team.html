Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our partnership and deployments.

How are the funds utilized?

Donations help offset essential travel and training costs for students and support community-identified projects in Malawi, such as surgical center renovations and pediatric ward improvements.

Is there a long-term commitment?

Yes. Unlike many "mission" trips, we maintain a year-round partnership with our host facilities to ensure continuity of care and ethical engagement.
