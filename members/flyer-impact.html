17 Medical Students
April 2026 Service-Learning Trip

In April 2026, a team of 17 medical students from Marshall University will travel to rural northern Malawi for a 3.5-week service-learning trip. Our focus is on learning from Malawian clinicians and communities, building cultural humility, and contributing to sustainable healthcare partnerships that bridge Appalachia and the "Warm Heart of Africa."
