Our Initiative

In April 2026, a team of 17 medical students from Marshall University will travel to Ekwendeni, Malawi. We are committed to learning from Malawian clinicians and contributing to sustainable healthcare partnerships.

Your support helps fund essential community-identified projects:

  • Dr. Chirwa's Surgical Center
  • Pediatric Ward Painting
  • Ekwendeni School for the Blind
  • Malaria Education & Research
