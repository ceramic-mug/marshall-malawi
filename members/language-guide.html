Language Basics

Chitumbuka Guide by Thomas Mosman

Primary Dialect Northern Malawi
Thomas's Note

"Chitumbuka / Tumbuka: Dialect of northern Malawi, parts of Zambia and Tanzania. Shares similarities with Chichewa (national language of Malawi)."

Pronunciation: All words pronounced phonetically (except the letter L is inconsistently pronounced as R).

Disclaimer

"This dictionary is limited only in what I was able to obtain from locals. I found little pattern in the language, and outside knowledge on translation is incredibly limited. This is imperfect but one of the more complete English-Chitumbuka dictionaries in the world. Good luck!"
